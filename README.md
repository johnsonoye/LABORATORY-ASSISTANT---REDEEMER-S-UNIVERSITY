## SCHOOL-BASED ACTIVITIES 3:
As **a Laboratory Assistant / Practical Demonstrator**, I'm a professionally competent, safety-conscious, and student-focused individual who plays a critical role in supporting teaching, learning, and research within the laboratory environment. The following outlines my key attributes and responsibilities in detail:

### 1. Academic and Technical Competence

* Possesses strong foundational knowledge of the relevant discipline (e.g., Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Environmental Science).
* Demonstrates proficiency in laboratory techniques such as sample preparation, titration, spectroscopy, chromatography, microscopy, and basic data analysis.
* Understands experimental design and can clearly link theory to practical applications.
* Keeps up-to-date with modern laboratory methods, instrumentation, and best practices.

---

### **2. Practical Demonstration Skills**

* Effectively demonstrates laboratory procedures step-by-step with clarity and precision.
* Explains the purpose, principles, and expected outcomes of experiments in simple, scientific terms.
* Guides undergraduate and postgraduate students during practical sessions, ensuring correct technique and interpretation of results.
* Assists in assessment of practical reports, logbooks, and continuous assessments where required.

---

### **3. Laboratory Preparation and Management**

* Prepares reagents, solutions, specimens, and equipment accurately ahead of practical sessions.
* Ensures laboratories are well-organized, functional, and adequately supplied.
* Manages inventory, chemical storage, labeling, and waste disposal in compliance with institutional and regulatory standards.
* Maintains and calibrates laboratory instruments, reporting faults promptly.

---

### **4. Health, Safety, and Environmental Compliance**

* Enforces laboratory safety rules and standard operating procedures (SOPs).
* Conducts risk assessments and ensures safe handling of chemicals, biological materials, and equipment.
* Promotes good laboratory practice (GLP), emergency preparedness, and environmental sustainability.
* Ensures proper use of personal protective equipment (PPE) by all laboratory users.

---

### **5. Research and Academic Support**

* Provides technical assistance to academic staff and researchers during experiments and fieldwork.
* Supports undergraduate projects, final-year research, MSc, and PhD laboratory work.
* Assists in data collection, basic statistical analysis, and documentation.
* Contributes to research outputs through reliable experimental support and accurate record-keeping.

---

### **6. Communication and Interpersonal Skills**

* Communicates clearly and professionally with students, lecturers, and technical staff.
* Shows patience, approachability, and mentorship, especially during practical sessions.
* Encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and scientific curiosity among students.
* Works collaboratively within multidisciplinary academic teams.

---

### **7. Documentation and Quality Assurance**

* Maintains detailed laboratory records, reagent logs, equipment service logs, and safety documentation.
* Supports quality control, accreditation, and audit processes.
* Ensures accuracy, reproducibility, and integrity of laboratory results.

---

### **8. Professional Ethics and Attitude**

* Demonstrates integrity, reliability, and respect for academic standards.
* Maintains confidentiality of research data and student information.
* Exhibits discipline, time management, and attention to detail.
* Shows willingness for continuous professional development through training and workshops.

---

### **9. Teaching Support and Innovation**

* Assists lecturers in developing practical manuals and laboratory guides.
* Incorporates digital tools, simulations, and modern teaching aids where appropriate.
* Adapts laboratory activities to accommodate diverse learners and resource constraints.

---
