## SCHOOL-BASED ACTIVITIES 3:
As **a Laboratory Assistant / Practical Demonstrator**, I'm a professionally competent, safety-conscious, and student-focused individual who plays a critical role in supporting teaching, learning, and research within the laboratory environment. The following outlines my key attributes and responsibilities in detail:

### 1. Academic and Technical Competence

* I possess strong foundational knowledge of the relevant discipline (e.g., Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Environmental Science).
* I demonstrate proficiency in laboratory techniques such as sample preparation, titration, spectroscopy, chromatography, microscopy, and basic data analysis.
* I understand experimental design and can clearly link theory to practical applications.
* I keep up-to-date with modern laboratory methods, instrumentation, and best practices.

### **2. Practical Demonstration Skills**

* I effectively demonstrate laboratory procedures step-by-step with clarity and precision.
* I explain the purpose, principles, and expected outcomes of experiments in simple, scientific terms.
* I guide undergraduate and postgraduate students during practical sessions, ensuring correct technique and interpretation of results.
* I assist in the assessment of practical reports, logbooks, and continuous assessments where required.

### **3. Laboratory Preparation and Management**

* I prepare reagents, solutions, specimens, and equipment accurately ahead of practical sessions.
* I ensure laboratories are well-organized, functional, and adequately supplied.
* I manage inventory, chemical storage, labelling, and waste disposal in compliance with institutional and regulatory standards.
* I maintain and calibrate laboratory instruments, reporting faults promptly.

### **4. Health, Safety, and Environmental Compliance**

* I enforce laboratory safety rules and standard operating procedures (SOPs).
* I conduct risk assessments and ensure safe handling of chemicals, biological materials, and equipment.
* I promote good laboratory practice (GLP), emergency preparedness, and environmental sustainability.
* I ensure proper use of personal protective equipment (PPE) by all laboratory users.

### **5. Research and Academic Support**

* I provide technical assistance to academic staff and researchers during experiments and fieldwork.
* I support undergraduate projects, final-year research, MSc, and PhD laboratory work.
* I assist in data collection, basic statistical analysis, and documentation.
* I contribute to research outputs through reliable experimental support and accurate record-keeping.

### **6. Communication and Interpersonal Skills**

* I communicate clearly and professionally with students, lecturers, and technical staff.
* I show patience, approachability, and mentorship, especially during practical sessions.
* I encourage critical thinking, problem-solving, and scientific curiosity among students.
* I work collaboratively within multidisciplinary academic teams.

### **7. Documentation and Quality Assurance**

* I maintain detailed laboratory records, reagent logs, equipment service logs, and safety documentation.
* I support quality control, accreditation, and audit processes.
* I ensure accuracy, reproducibility, and integrity of laboratory results.

### **8. Professional Ethics and Attitude**

* I demonstrate integrity, reliability, and respect for academic standards.
* I maintain confidentiality of research data and student information.
* I exhibit discipline, time management, and attention to detail.
* I show willingness for continuous professional development through training and workshops.

### **9. Teaching Support and Innovation**

* I assist lecturers in developing practical manuals and laboratory guides.
* I incorporate digital tools, simulations, and modern teaching aids where appropriate.
* I adapt laboratory activities to accommodate diverse learners and resource constraints.
